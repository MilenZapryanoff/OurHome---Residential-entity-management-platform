OurHome is a digital platform designed to assist current and future building managers who have chosen the platform as their primary helper!

OurHome is not a building manager but its closest friend!


All our efforts are focused on providing completely free and accessible tools to simplify the work of every building manager, regardless of their level of experience.


To achieve this, we have developed numerous features and options for individual customization of each digital condominium. These tools make managing processes within your building easier and more efficient.


The platform is intended for all building managers and property owners. Through it, every property owner can quickly and easily check all important matters in the building, including due fees, events, reports of irregularities, and more.

To create a digital condominium (DC), the building manager must register their condominium in the platform.

After successfully registering the DC, you will gain access to the features we've developed to help you manage your condominium.

After completing the initial setup and configurations, you can invite property owners/users in your condominium to register in the system.

To register as a property owner/user in the platform, a registered digital condominium is required.

If your condominium is already registered, you should have received the necessary details from your building manager to start your registration as a property owner/user.

If you do not have a digital condominium identifier and access code, we recommend contacting your building manager to obtain them.
